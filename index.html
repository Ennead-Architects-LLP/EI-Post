Meet Your Instructor

JULIUS BETILA

Julius Betila's natural curiosity in science and technology led to his interest in spirituality and soundbath meditation. When Julius first discovered sound as an energy modality, he was able to connect his various passions and pursuits to help others through his work.

With a loving intention and gratitude, Julius creates meditative soundscapes with a mixture of unique instruments to promote a sense of well-being and to strengthen the heart-brain coherence. In addition to leading sound meditation workshops in NYC offices with Exubrancy, Julius also teaches weekly sound meditation classes at Montauk Salt Cave in the East Village and works with senior citizens and children in Queens and Brooklyn.
